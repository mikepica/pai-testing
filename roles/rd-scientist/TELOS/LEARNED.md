# Learned

## L1: Protocol changes invalidate historical comparisons
After the 2019 protocol update, three months of work comparing pre/post data had to be discarded. Lesson: always confirm measurement consistency before any longitudinal analysis.

## L2: False negatives are catastrophically more expensive than false positives
A false positive wastes lab time (weeks). A false negative lets a toxic compound advance (years, millions, patient harm). Every AI evaluation must be weighted accordingly.

## L3: "The data is clean" is almost always an assumption
