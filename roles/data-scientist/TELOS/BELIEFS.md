# Beliefs

- A model is only as good as its training data — garbage in, garbage out is not a cliche, it's a law
- Explainability and accuracy are not opposites — they're design choices with real tradeoffs
- The biggest bottleneck in pharma AI is data access, not algorithms
- A family of specialized models beats one general model every time
- If you can't explain your model to a biologist, your model isn't ready for production
